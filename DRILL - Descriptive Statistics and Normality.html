In [4]:
gamma = np.random.gamma(5,0.5, 100)
plt.hist(gamma)
plt.axvline(gamma.mean(), color='b', linestyle='solid', linewidth=2)
plt.axvline(gamma.mean() + gamma.std(), color='b', linestyle='dashed', linewidth=2)
plt.axvline(gamma.mean()-gamma.std(), color='b', linestyle='dashed', linewidth=2)
plt.show()

The mean is no longer a measure of "central" tendency, as it does not fall in the center, and the standard deviation no longer describes how much variance there is. Asymmetric probability distributions are described as "skewed."
